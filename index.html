Tata Tiago Petrol (2026):

The 2026 Tata Tiago Petrol comes with a refreshed design, updated interiors, and modern features such as a larger touchscreen infotainment system and improved safety equipment. It continues to offer a fuel-efficient petrol engine, making it a practical and affordable city hatchback.

Tata Tiago EV (2026):

The 2026 Tata Tiago EV gets a redesigned exterior, upgraded cabin, and two battery options offering a claimed range of up to 285 km. With fast-charging support, connected-car features, and a starting price from ₹6.99 lakh, it remains one of India's most affordable electric cars.

Why Tiago ?

The Tata Tiago is a standout choice for budget-conscious buyers seeking uncompromised safety, solid build quality, and excellent fuel efficiency. Starting at a highly competitive price point, it is ideal for navigating city traffic while offering the robust durability Tata is known for.Choosing the Tata Tiago offers several distinct advantages:Top-Tier Safety: It is one of the safest hatchbacks in its class. Built on a high-strength steel structure, it includes standard features like multiple airbags (including six in recent facelifts), ABS with EBD, and corner stability control.Reliable Performance: Equipped with a refined 1.2-litre petrol engine, it provides strong low-end torque for smooth city driving. It is also available in Petrol, EV, and iCNG (including a dual-cylinder CNG AMT) options to suit diverse driving needs.Low Running Costs: The Tiago delivers impressive mileage, keeping your daily fuel budget in check. The CNG variant pushes efficiency even further without sacrificing boot space thanks to a twin-cylinder layout.Modern Features: Despite its affordable price, it comes packed with tech like a 7-inch touchscreen, Android Auto and Apple CarPlay, premium Harman audio systems, and a digital instrument cluster.City-Friendly: With a compact footprint and small turning radius, it is incredibly easy to maneuver through dense traffic and tight parking spots.
